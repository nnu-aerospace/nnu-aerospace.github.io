# KAUIDA - AMBER

KAUIDA - AMBER was a joint project between the University of Hawaii and NNU. KAUIDA was built for the 2020 RockSat-X year, however everything was delayed a year due to Covid. AMBER was a add-on to KAUIDA by two NNU students for their senior design project

## Summary

The project focused on:

- Extending a 360 cam for recording VR usable video
- RF tag testing
- GPS and environmental recording devices
- Plastic thermal stress testing

## Results

The extandable boom with the 360 cam ripped off during re entry, the RF tag stuff didn't quite work as expected, the GPS system suffered a minor error. The plastic test bench survived
