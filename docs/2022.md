# MARSHA

Marsha Explored Robotic Arms in Space and Soft Robotics

## Summary

The project focused on:

- Deep Reinforcement Learning
- Robotic Arms in Space
- Soft Robotics

## Results

MARSHA successfully grabbed a ball and threw it. Afterwards, however, it jammed up and locked the state machine. No camera footage was recorded and most of the project fell off on re-entry.
