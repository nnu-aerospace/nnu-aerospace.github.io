# DAEDALUS VII

DAEDALUS VII continued the work of DAEDALUS IV, redesigning the arm to function properly and building a new ball holder

## Summary

The project focused on:

- Robotic Arms in Space
- End-Mounted Depth Cameras
- Mechanical Ball Launcher

## Results

The arm shifted during launch, so didn't extract from the payload area. The ball launcher mostly worked, and all camera systems performed admirably.
