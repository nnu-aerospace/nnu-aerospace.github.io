# DAEDALUS

DAEDALUS continued the robotic arm research of MARSHA

## Summary

The project focused on:

- Robotic Arms in Space

## Results

A few minutes after launch, the sounding rocket started oscillating and broke apart. The payload crashed and burned on the beach. Later analysis showed that the fins on the second stage booster weren't aligned properly. Two were angled appropriately, a third was at too shallow of an angle, and the fourth was angled backwards

## Final Report

Because of the crash, no final report is available for this year
